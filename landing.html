The Problem

Discharge is Broken.
We Fixed It.

In major hospitals, discharge delays cascade into bed shortages, staff burnout, and rising patient costs — all caused by fragmented, manual workflows.

🏥

Bed Shortages

Delayed discharges block new admissions, creating critical capacity crises during peak hours across major hospitals.

😫

Staff Burnout

Nurses and doctors spend hours on manual coordination — checklists, calls, follow-ups — instead of direct patient care.

💸

Higher Costs

Inefficient handoffs between pharmacy, billing, and transport inflate costs for both hospitals and patients.

Core Features

Not Reactive. Not Rule-Based.
Truly Autonomous.

VHeal AI operates as a digital hospital staff member — reasoning, delegating, and communicating across every department.

📍

Discharge Detection

Automatically triggers when a patient is marked ready — validating vitals, medications, labs, and required signatures.

🧾

AI-Generated Summaries

Produces structured discharge summaries powered by GPT-4o, Claude 3, and Mistral — contextual and accurate.

📞

Smart Dept. Alerts

Sends WhatsApp, email, and SMS notifications to pharmacy, billing, transport, and nursing automatically.

📊

Live Dashboard

Real-time visibility into every agent action, staff update, and discharge stage across all active patients.

🧠

Multi-Agent Planning

CrewAI + LangChain orchestration enables multi-step reasoning and task delegation across specialized agents.

📆

Discharge Scheduling

Coordinates room availability, transport booking, and staff calendars to execute discharges without friction.

How It Works

From Trigger to Discharge
in Minutes

01

Patient Marked Ready

Doctor marks patient "Ready for Discharge" in the EMR. VHeal AI triggers autonomously.

02

Multi-Step Validation

Agent validates vitals, pending labs, medication clearances, and required signatures via API calls.

03

Department Coordination

Delegates tasks to pharmacy, billing, transport, and nursing via WhatsApp, email, and SMS.

04

Summary Generation

GPT-4o generates a complete, structured discharge summary and sends it to the patient's portal.

05

Patient Discharged

Bed is marked available, analytics updated, and all staff notified. Zero manual steps.
